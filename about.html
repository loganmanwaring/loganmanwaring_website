About Me

A little bit about me!

I grew up in San Antonio, Texas with my family. I am the 2nd oldest of 4, and I have two sisters and one brother. I love anything outdoors including hiking, fishing, camping, basketball, and all other kinds of sports.

After I graduated high school in 2021, I served a 2 year LDS mission in Sweden. I loved it, and I had a wonderful experince, and I was able to learn a lot and develop a lot of really cool relationships with the people there. Upon returning home, I started school at BYU in 2023, and I have really enjoyed it since.

I have decided that I want to study finance, and I will be applying to the major this summer. During my time here at BYU, I was fortunate enough to meet my now fiancee, Heidi. She is the love of my life, and she is perfect for me. We met playing pickleball, and we enjoy being outside together, playing sports, trying new things, going new places, and much more. We are so excited to get married this August and start our lives together!
